Delicious B&B: Decor & Match 3

Turn Your Dream B&B into Reality! Match, Decorate, and Serve Happiness!

About the Game

Delicious B&B: Decor & Match 3 combines the charm of renovating your dream bed-and-breakfast with the excitement of match-3 puzzles. Step into a cozy countryside setting, where you’ll help Emily, an aspiring innkeeper, restore an old B&B, design its interiors, and serve delicious meals to delighted guests.

Every match-3 puzzle you solve earns resources and decorations to upgrade your B&B. From creating gourmet dishes to building a stunning garden patio, the possibilities are endless. Experience a heartwarming story filled with quirky characters, charming moments, and a dash of culinary magic.

Unique Feature: Guest Stories

Introduce an interactive storytelling element where every guest has a unique backstory, preferences, and requests. As you serve them and upgrade your B&B, you’ll unlock special dialogue, mini-quests, and surprising rewards. From a traveling writer searching for inspiration to a honeymooning couple looking for romance, your guests bring life and depth to the game.

How to Play

  • Match Tiles

    Drag and swap to align three or more identical tiles, clearing them and completing level objectives.

  • Earn Rewards

    Match combos to unlock powerful boosters like "Tea Kettles" or "Cupcake Bombs."

  • Decorate Your B&B

    Use rewards to select furniture styles, wall art, and landscaping options to make your inn unique.

  • Unlock New Content

    Progress through levels to reveal new rooms, gardens, and storylines for your B&B.
